# Analytos ICP — Ideal Customer Profile (Internal)

**Applies to:** Stockly, Inspectly (manufacturing suite)
**Owner:** Narayan Laksham
**Last reviewed:** internal

## Segment 1 — Mid-Market Discrete Manufacturers (direct)

**Firmographics**
- Revenue: **$50M–$500M**
- Employees: 100–2,000; 1–6 plants
- Sectors (priority order): precision machining, medical device manufacturing, industrial equipment, packaging, automotive tier-2 suppliers, electronics assembly, aerospace suppliers
- ERP footprint: **NetSuite or SAP Business One** preferred (fastest integration); Epicor/Infor acceptable
- Geography: US first (Midwest/Southeast manufacturing belts), then EU

**Trigger signals (what makes them ready now)**
- Still running kanban on physical cards or Excel min/max (Stockly)
- Inventory write-downs or working-capital pressure from board/PE owner (Stockly)
- Hiring posts for inventory planners or quality engineers — headcount pain (both)
- ISO 13485 / AS9100 certified or pursuing certification, with manual FAI/inspection documentation (Inspectly)
- Recent NetStock evaluation or churn (Stockly displacement)

## Segment 2 — PE Firms & Operating Partners (multiplier channel)

- Lower-middle-market PE funds with **3+ manufacturing portfolio companies**
- Pitch: **"build once, deploy across the portfolio"** — one POC at a lighthouse portfolio company, then standardized rollout
- Buyer: Operating Partner / Portfolio Ops lead; sponsor: portfolio company CFO
- Value framing: EBITDA improvement via working capital release (Stockly) and quality-team productivity (Inspectly); perpetual license option avoids adding recurring SaaS cost to the P&L

## Personas

| Persona | Role in deal | Cares about | Losing message | Winning message |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Plant Manager** | Champion (Stockly) | Stockouts, expediting chaos, floor discipline | "AI transformation" | "Fewer stockouts, less firefighting, your planners get their week back" |
| **Supply Chain Director** | Champion/buyer (Stockly) | Inventory turns, working capital | Feature lists | "21% inventory reduction in 90 days at a shop like yours" |
| **Quality Manager / Engineer** | Champion (Inspectly) | Audit readiness, documentation backlog | "Replace your team" | "4–6 hours per inspection plan down to 20 minutes, engineer stays in the loop" |
| **CFO / PE Operating Partner** | Economic buyer | EBITDA, capex vs opex, risk | Vague ROI | Working capital release + perpetual license, 2-week POC proof |

## Disqualifiers

- Process/continuous manufacturing (chemicals, food) — poor fit for Pull Kanban v1
- Revenue < $30M (deal size too small) or Fortune 500 (procurement cycle too long for our stage)
- Homegrown ERP with no API access
